Color Schemes

Monochromatic

Uses different tones, shades, and tints within a specific hue

Triadic

3 colors on a wheel that are about as far as they can be from each other with (near/almost) equal distance apart. A '60-30-10' rule is usually applied when put to use.

Analogous

Three colors next to each other on a color wheel
